Highlights !

Saina Nehwal out of Rio Olympics 2016 after shock loss to Ukraine's Maria Ulitina. Saina Nehwal faced tough resistance from Brazilian player Lohaynny Vicente but did just about enough at crucial junctures to post a win on the start of the tournament. She won 21-17, 21-17 in a match that lasted 39 minutes.

Aditi ended her campaign at the 41st spot in the women's golf event. Aditi Ashok's exploits prove India's golf future is in safe hands.Aditi was born on 29 March 1998 in Bangalore. She studied at the The Frank Anthony Public School in Bangalore and graduated in 2016.[

After her historic fourth place finish in the finals of vault event at Rio Olympics 2016, one would think that Indian gymnast Dipa Karmakar has earned a well-deserved break. However, the 23-year-old had other, more important plans. Just a day after returning to Agartala, Dipa appeared for her second semester MA exams at the Distance Learning Centre of Tripura University.
